SCL Group

The Seoul Clinical Laboratories (SCL) was launched in 1983 as a medical institution specializing in clinical trials. Since the foundation, SCL has consistently pursued the three core values of quality, service and R&D. In 1997, the reliability of our clinical trials was recognized by obtaining certification form an international control institution, CAP. Since then, we have established ourselves as one of the nation’s best reference laboratories concerning diagnostic examination, pathology examination, special analysis and genetic diagnosis with the globalization of sample testing through the business partnership with LSIM in Japan, Mayo Clinic and Quest Diagnostics in the U.S., and Dian Lab in China.

In addition, a number of our research papers have been published every year in the prestigious scientific journals thanks to our continuous research activities.

Based on these continuous R&D efforts, SCL will provide the highest level of research services for clients, and all our researchers, including SCL specialists, will provide accurate inspection services through international quality control systems for all processes from inspection requests to reporting results.

As the most professional committed research institution in Korea, SCL will continuously do our best by putting our customers first and become a patient-oriented international clinical research institution by studying state-of-the-art medicine based on evidence.

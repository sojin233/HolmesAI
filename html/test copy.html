IheartUTM

(Digital Therapeutics)

IheartUTM (DTx; Digital Therapeutics) is software-based interventions crafted to treat, manage, or prevent medical conditions. These interventions are versatile, able to be delivered across a range of devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables, making healthcare more accessible and personalized
